LeMans Porsche

Porsche is the most successful manufacturer in 24 Hours of Le Mans history, boasting 19 overall victories and over 100 class wins since their debut in 1951. Key eras include the dominant 917 in the early 1970s, the 956/962 Group C reign (1980s), and the 919 Hybrid hat-trick (2015-2017).
